What we do

Cipher Principles Fund is an open-ended cryptocurrency fund designed to maximise returns through an actively managed, diversified portfolio of digital assets.

Discretionary Management is the heart of what we do; building an actively managed portfolio on three fundamental approaches:

Fundamental approach

We follow a buy-and-hold strategy intermittently rebalancing the portfolio after the in-depth analysis of economic and financial drivers of cryptocurrencies, both qualitative and quantitative.

Systematic approach

We trade digital assets on a variety of cryptocurrency exchanges using active trading strategies like leveraged trading, price arbitrage, algorithmic trading and a number of others.

Intrinsic value approach

We invest in digital assets through identifying opportunities with balanced risk and reward profile and analyzing of the tech basis, the management, the product and the coin dimension.

Looking for a managed account?

For clients who either prefer or require more control over their individual investment choices, Cipher Principles has a dedicated team who can give direction and proactive advice based on market trends and analysis.

With your mandate we will make investment decisions on your behalf. Maximising returns based on goals you’ve defined, giving you control to decide which funds you participate in.

How we do it

Secured by science

Our team of data scientists work on the development of cryptocurrency investment strategies. (Optimal exit strategies, efficiency of arbitrage, price drivers, forecast models and optimization of cryptocurrency-based portfolios with regard to liquidity constraints).

How we do it

Regulated custody & execution

How we do it

Strategic investment infrastructure

A better investment management infrastructure, which includes: Corporate accounts on cryptocurrency exchanges, selected trading platforms, supervised digital asset storage methods, portfolio valuation confirmed by a regulated administrator & portfolio.

Why we do it

A confluence of skill and experience in a time of opportunity

We recognise that cryptocurrencies are more than just a new financial product. They are an entirely new technology that will change the way people do business and the way countries trade with each other. But with this enormous shift looming on the horizon, there is a seemingly endless stream of fear, uncertainty, and doubt casting shadows over the incredible potential waiting to be unlocked.

The team we have built is designed specifically to cut through all of that; to evaluate each investment opportunity on the merits of the technology it rests on; and to make the right investment decisions at the right time, for the right portfolios.

We want to see a financial services world that is efficient, transparent, and exciting for every person – not just governments and large archaic financial institutions.

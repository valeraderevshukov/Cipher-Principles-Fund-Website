What we do

Cipher Principles Fund is an open-ended cryptocurrency fund designed to maximise returns through an actively managed, diversified portfolio of digital assets.

Discretionary Management is the heart of what we do; building an actively managed portfolio on three fundamental approaches:

Fundamental approach

We follow a buy-and-hold strategy intermittently rebalancing the portfolio after the in-depth analysis of economic and financial drivers of cryptocurrencies, both qualitative and quantitative.

Systematic approach

We trade digital assets on a variety of cryptocurrency exchanges using active trading strategies like leveraged trading, price arbitrage, algorithmic trading and a number of others.

Intrinsic value approach

We invest in digital assets through identifying opportunities with balanced risk and reward profile and analyzing of the tech basis, the management, the product and the coin dimension.

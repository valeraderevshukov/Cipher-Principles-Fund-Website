The evolution of the cryptocurrency market follows the same fundamental principles as traditional financial markets. At the same time, cryptocurrencies possess certain idiosyncrasies. For this reason, the practical implementation of tried and tested procedures used within traditional financial markets, fail to yield positive results.

Today, the most significant research problems in the field of cryptocurrencies, include finding the optimal exit strategies, examining efficiency of arbitrage, identifying current price drivers, as well as building forecast models and optimizing cryptocurrency-based portfolios while taking into account liquidity constraints.
